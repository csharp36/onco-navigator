IMPRESSION

1. Right upper lobe spiculated mass measuring 3.4 x 2.8 x 3.1 cm, highly

2. Enlarged right paratracheal (4R), subcarinal (7), and right hilar (10R)

   intrapulmonary lymph node. Follow-up per Fleischner Society guidelines if
   clinically indicated.

4. No evidence of distant metastatic disease in the visualized chest and upper
   abdomen.

RECOMMENDATION
