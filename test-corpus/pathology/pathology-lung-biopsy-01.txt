GROSS DESCRIPTION
-----------------
Received in formalin, labeled "right upper lobe lung biopsy," are four
cylindrical cores of tan-white tissue, each measuring 0.8-1.2 cm in length and
0.1 cm in diameter. All tissue submitted in cassettes A1-A2.

MICROSCOPIC DESCRIPTION
-----------------------
Sections demonstrate cores of pulmonary parenchyma extensively involved by a
moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma. The tumor exhibits predominantly
acinar and papillary growth patterns with focal lepidic component. Tumor cells
show enlarged, hyperchromatic nuclei with prominent nucleoli and moderate
amounts of eosinophilic cytoplasm. Scattered mitotic figures are identified.
Necrosis is present in approximately 10% of the examined tissue. The background
lung parenchyma shows mild chronic inflammation and focal emphysematous change.

No definite lymphovascular invasion is identified in the limited biopsy
material.
